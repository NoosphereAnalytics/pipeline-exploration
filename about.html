What is “Noosphere Analytics”?

This company is currently a single proprietorship LLC owned by Morgan Foster. Including her time working for Electronic Warfare Associates (2009-2013) and the Army Research Laboratory (2009) during university, Morgan has 16 years of experience building software systems as a technology professional. Notably she has also worked on data infrastructure (Facebook, Box), compiler and static analysis implementation (Mozilla, Twitter, Google) and automating workflows via Machine Learning (Google).

For example, she re-implemented Array sorting in the SpiderMonkey JavaScript Engine (notably used in Bloomberg terminals at the time) leading to a 5x improvement in performance: link to Morgan's diff. She also implemented many features from and participated in global discussions about the ECMA standard. Particularly around the way that dates are parsed across web browsers. She is provided as a reference here. (Note: “Phillips” is Morgan’s maiden name).

You can also see she was among the earliest contributors to a key component of the flagship WebAssembly runtime environment: wasmtime.

These experiences have allowed her to build a diverse skill set which lends itself well to the problem of building a “smart data pipeline”. That is, a data pipeline with autonomous components such that they are able to locate and process data that would have historically required human oversight to reach. Case in point, school board documents which are published in myriad formats and hosted across a diverse array of platforms which vary wildly in terms of sophistication and accessibility.

Morgan is passionate about this project through volunteer work in her local community, serving on the board of directors for the Whatcom Dispute Resolution Center in Bellingham, Washington. She noticed heated discussions in the community around Title IX implementations where school officials appeared to vilify certain groups of students. When later, nearby to her home, a student from this group was attacked she decided to investigate how widespread the issue was and found a surprising lack of clarity via data. She built the original prototype of this system in order to solve her own desire to collect that data and build that clarity.

Partnership & Collaboration

Drawing on her extensive background in strategic alliances and partner marketing—with roles at Confluent, Deloitte, and beyond—Erin leverages her network to introduce Noosphere Analytics to potential clients and investors.
